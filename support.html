Frequently Asked Questions

1. How do I add a new student inquiry?

You can add a new student inquiry by navigating to the "Manage Inquiries" section and clicking the "Add New Inquiry" button.

2. How do I change the admin settings?

Go to the "Settings" tab in the sidebar. You can modify your account details and application settings there.

3. How can I contact support for further assistance?

You can fill out the form below to send a message directly to the support team.
